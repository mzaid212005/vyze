Immediate Self-Help Strategies

Grounding Techniques
  • 5-4-3-2-1: Name 5 things you can see, 4 you can touch, 3 you can hear, 2 you can smell, 1 you can taste
  • Deep breathing: Inhale for 4 counts, hold for 4, exhale for 4
  • Hold ice cubes or splash cold water on your face
Distraction Techniques
  • Call or text a trusted friend or family member
  • Go for a walk or engage in light exercise
  • Listen to calming music or a favorite podcast
